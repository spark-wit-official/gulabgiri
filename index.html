Benefits of rose water

  • Hydrates the skin
  • Gently removes excess dirt and oil
  • Helps soothe irritated skin
  • Great for sensitive skin
  • Helps enhance mood
  • pH balancing
  • Helps prevent and reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines
  • Helps to prevent and reduce acne
  • Helps minimize the appearance of large pores

Uses of rose water

  • Toner : Use as a toner after cleansing your face twice a day regularly. Doing this will provide your skin with the hydration it needs, making it glow.
  • Refresh your makeup : Keep a spray bottle full of rose water on hand! It not only rehydrates your skin, but a few sprays can freshen up your makeup as well.
  • Face mask : Add rose water to our Multani Mitti clay to make a past. Use once a week if you have oily skin. For any other skin types use every 2 weeks.
  • Soothe razor burn : Spray on some rose water after shaving to minimize redness and itching.
